Private Desert Tour

Experience the magic of the Moroccan desert in a romantic setting

This unforgettable experience will start in the medina in Marrakech. We will visit the Dar Al Bacha Palace (Museum of Confluences). The Dar Al Bacha is the medina's finest examples of riad architecture, and it also holds temporary exhibits highlighting the different facets of Moroccan culture. The museum also houses an opulent period cafe specializing in the finest arabica coffee. Following the museum visit we will tour the souk and have lunch in the medina. After lunch we will visit a hammam and spa for a couples massage.

After the spa, we will head out to our next destination that will take place in a luxury desert camp in the Agafay Rocky Desert 40 km (25 miles) from Marrakech. You will have a chance to relax and spend a few hours enjoying the magical Sahara experience. Here you will enjoy a camel ride followed by a romantic sunset dinner in the camp. After dinner you will be transported back to your hotel.
